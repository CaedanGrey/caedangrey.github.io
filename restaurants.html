The Ocean's Bounty

Seaside Dining with a Fresh Twist

At the water's edge, The Ocean's Bounty offers a menu that changes with the tides, specializing in the freshest local fish and seafood.

The Treetop Terrace

Tropical Flavors in the Canopy

Elevated among the rainforest's canopy, The Treetop Terrace serves a fusion of Tanitian cuisine and international flavors.

The Lava Grill

Sizzling Delights with a Volcanic View

Located with a view of Taniti's active volcano, The Lava Grill is renowned for its open-flame cooking techniques.

The Starlit Luau

An Authentic Tanitian Feast Under the Stars

Experience traditional Tanitian culture at The Starlit Luau, where every meal is a celebration with local dishes and live island music.

The Colonial House

Classic Elegance Meets Modern American Cuisine

Set in a restored colonial mansion, The Colonial House offers American-style meals with a sophisticated twist, blending local ingredients with classic recipes.

The Pan-Asian Pavilion

Exquisite Flavors from Across Asia

The Pan-Asian Pavilion presents a culinary journey through Asia, from the spice-laden markets of Southeast Asia to the delicate flavors of Japanese cuisine.

The Citrus Grove

Healthful Eats in a Citrus Orchard

Nestled within a grove of citrus trees, The Citrus Grove specializes in organic, health-conscious meals with freshly squeezed juices and smoothies.

The Sunset Bistro

French-Inspired Fare with a Tanitian Twist

The Sunset Bistro offers a romantic dining experience with a menu that combines French culinary techniques with the exotic ingredients of Taniti.

The Mermaid's Grotto

Underwater Ambiance and Seafood Delights

Dine beneath the waves at The Mermaid's Grotto, an underwater restaurant offering an array of seafood specialties in a unique environment.

The Yacht Club

Exclusive Dining with a Nautical Flair

Accessible only to members and their guests, The Yacht Club offers an exclusive dining experience with a view of the harbor.
