The Taniti Serenity Resort

Luxury Amidst the Waves

Immerse yourself in the pinnacle of luxury at The Taniti Serenity Resort. Nestled on the edge of a pristine sandy beach, the resort boasts panoramic ocean views, private balconies, and an exclusive spa experience.

The Rainforest Retreat

Eco-Luxury in the Heart of the Jungle

For those who seek harmony with nature, the Rainforest Retreat offers eco-friendly accommodations set deep within Taniti's verdant rainforests.

The Volcanic Vista Inn

Adventure at Your Doorstep

Perched on the slopes of Taniti's active volcano, the Volcanic Vista Inn provides a thrilling escape for adventure-loving couples.

Merriton Landing B&B

Charm and Comfort by the Bay

Experience genuine Tanitian hospitality at Merriton Landing Bed and Breakfast. This family-run establishment in the heart of the bustling Merriton Landing offers cozy, well-appointed rooms.

The Harbor Hideaway

Secluded Romance by the Harbor

For couples seeking intimacy, the Harbor Hideaway offers secluded cottages dotting the small but safe harbor of Taniti.

The Artist's Alcove

Inspiration and Island Elegance

Located near Taniti City's vibrant art galleries and cultural hubs, The Artist's Alcove is a boutique hotel that celebrates the island's artistic spirit.
